Meet Our Leadership

Karen Weathermon

Director

First-Year Programs

kweathermon@wsu.edu

Weathermon is passionate about, and long experienced in, the field of education. With a background that includes secondary and university teaching, she has developed and/or directed a number of WSU programs over a quarter-century that serve all undergraduates, regardless of major or grade. The foundation for her work has been her conviction that learning is about building connections.

Leslie Jo Sena

Assistant Director

First-Year Programs

senal@wsu.edu

Sena has devoted more than a decade of her professional career in education to first-year and other students at WSU. She taught several courses in both English and writing, and is increasingly and extensively involved in building out the First-Year Experience, part of WSU Undergraduate Education. The opportunity to serve as both a faculty member and administrator aligns with two philosophies that guide her: education is a tool for personal and societal growth, and “the first year matters.”
