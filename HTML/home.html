Cougars: Transition. Community. Connection.

Experience what it really means to become a Cougar. Regardless of major, there are some keys every first-year student needs for a successful experience at Washington State and beyond. WSU's caring experts and specialized programs help you find those keys and thrive in college.

Aim for Good Grades

You're a college student now. Have an academic lifestyle.

  • Always go to class
  • Participate in the Common Reading program
  • Visit your instructor's office hours
  • Get a tutor: check ASCC, your residence hall, the Math Learning Center

Make Close Friends

Be a Cougar Leader

  • Get involved in your residence hall
  • Join a student club or Registered Student Organization
  • Start a study group

Stay Happy and Healthy

Maintain your well-being

  • Take a PEACT course
  • Participate in intramural sports
  • Check out Health and Wellness workshops on Time Management, Stress and Emotional Health, or Finances

Discover Your Own Path

Set your own direction.

  • Visit with your advisor
  • Drop by the Career Center at ASCC
  • Work with peer consultants at the Writing Center or Math Learning Center
  • Consider studying abroad
  • Explore undergraduate research and distinguished scholarships
